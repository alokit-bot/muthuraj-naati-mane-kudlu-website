Our Story

Authentic Naati flavours, served with family warmth

At Muthuraj Naati Mane, every plate carries the soul of namma Bengaluru. We are proud to serve traditional Naati (country-style) Karnataka dishes alongside a full multicuisine menu — from fiery Naati chicken curry and rich Mughlai specialties to comforting idli breakfasts and family thalis.

A welcoming, family-friendly space where good food brings people together. Loved by our neighbourhood with a 4.4 ⭐ rating across 625+ happy reviews — come taste why families keep coming back.
